Starting template

DDreamkol is a simple database-website based on the Shards template designed by Designrevision

The Idea

The main idea behind DDreamkol is to have all that you need about a village of the Dolni Drimkol valley in one place, avoiding to search all over the internet finding either a lot of pages of the same thing or none at all. We try to group all of those informations and organize them so you won't have a hard time finding what you are searching for.
This is meant to be an open site, so everyone can help achieving this goal which can be really hard for only one person.
